Altitude automatically classifies each banned product by risk level to help you prioritize your monitoring efforts.

**Priority: Periodic Checks**

- No injuries reported

## How Risk Classification Works

The system automatically calculates risk levels based on:
- Number of deaths reported
- Number of injuries reported
- Number of units affected
- Type of hazards involved
- Severity of potential harm

This classification helps you:
- Prioritize your team's limited time on HIGH-risk banned products
- Allocate resources effectively across your banned product management program
- Generate risk-based reports for regulatory oversight
- Focus enforcement efforts where they matter most
